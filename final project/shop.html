About Us

Welcome to Dave's Karenderya! We are passionate about bringing you the best culinary experiences. Our mission is to provide delicious meals made from the freshest ingredients, ensuring that every bite is a delight.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Dave's Karenderya started as a small kitchen with a big dream. Our founders, Dave Ejorango and John Benedict Vivas, wanted to create a place where people could enjoy authentic flavors and a warm atmosphere. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved local eatery, known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the freshest ingredients.
  • Community: We believe in giving back to our community.
  • Passion: Our love for food drives everything we do.

Join Us!

We invite you to visit us and experience the joy of good food. Whether you're dining in or ordering out, we promise to make your meal memorable.
